Slugs are friends!

They have many cool qualities.

And deserve our admiration.

What is a slug?

Slugs are terrestrial gastropod mollusc. Often referred to as shell-less.

Here is just a few things that make our little buddies so cool:

  • Slugs have a radula which has thousands of tiny little teeth.
  • Slugs breathe through a blowhole called a pneumostome.
  • Slugs produce two types of slime which is classified as liquid crystal.

Slugs have three stages of life.

  1. Egg
  2. Immature stage
  3. Adult

Slugs love to eat decaying matter, leaves,
dead plants, fruits, excrement,
and fungi!
